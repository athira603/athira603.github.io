Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 8, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Calco (“Formula Vault,” “we,” “our,” or “us”) collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application (the “App”) on Android, including through Google Play. By using the App, you agree to this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect or receive the following types of information:

  • Device and usage data: Device type, operating system, app version, and general usage (e.g., feature usage, crashes) to improve the App.
  • Data you provide: Content you create in the App (e.g., formulas, categories, calculation history) is stored locally on your device. We do not upload this content to our servers unless you use a feature that explicitly syncs or shares data.
  • Advertising-related data: Our advertising partners may collect device identifiers (such as advertising ID), IP address, and interaction with ads to show relevant ads and measure ad performance. See Section 3.
  • In-app purchase data: When you make in-app purchases, Google Play processes the transaction. We may receive non-sensitive purchase information (e.g., product ID, purchase time) to provide and restore purchases. We do not collect or store your payment card details. See Section 4.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the App and its features.
  • Display advertisements and measure ad performance (see Section 3).
  • Process and restore in-app purchases (see Section 4).
  • Analyze usage and fix bugs, and to comply with legal obligations.

3. Advertising

The App may show advertisements provided by third-party ad networks (such as Google AdMob). These partners may collect and use data including:

  • Advertising ID (e.g., Android Advertising ID)
  • Device information (e.g., type, OS version)
  • IP address and approximate location
  • Interactions with ads (e.g., views, clicks)

This data is used to deliver and personalize ads, prevent fraud, and measure effectiveness. You can limit ad tracking or reset your advertising ID in your device settings (e.g., Google account or Android advertising settings). For more information, see:

  • Google Advertising & Privacy

4. In-App Purchases

In-app purchases (e.g., premium features, remove ads) are processed by Google Play Billing. Google collects and processes payment information according to its own terms and privacy policy. We receive only information necessary to:

  • Unlock purchased content or features in the App.
  • Restore purchases when you reinstall the App or use a new device.

We do not store your payment card or financial details. For Google’s practices, see Google Privacy Policy.

We use services provided by Google and other third parties that may collect or process data, including:

  • Google Play Services – app distribution, updates, in-app billing.

These services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them.

We retain information only as long as needed to provide the App, comply with law, or enforce our terms. Data stored only on your device (e.g., formulas, history) remains until you clear app data or uninstall the App.
